## Notes on H3 Haplogroup

- **H3** is a subclade of mitochondrial haplogroup H, which is the most common mtDNA haplogroup in Europe
- **H3** is particularly common in Western Europe, especially in the Iberian Peninsula, France, and the British Isles
- **H3** is also found at moderate frequencies in Central and Eastern Europe
- The various subclades (H3a, H3w, H3g1, H3c) represent different branches within the H3 lineage, indicating different maternal lineages

**Geographic Distribution:**
- H3 is most frequent in Western Europe (up to 10-15% in some regions)
- Lower frequencies in Eastern Europe and Scandinavia
- Also found in North Africa and the Middle East at lower frequencies
